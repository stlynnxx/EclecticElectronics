Slippery Penguin
We are proud to offer a free, open source SUID enumeration tool for security professions to use known as Slippery Penguin.
Slippery Penguin is currently in a testing phase and we welcome your contributions! If you have any feature ideas or even just data from your own testing you'd be willing to share to make the tool more robust, please reach out or send over a pull request via Github!
